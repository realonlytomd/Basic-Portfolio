About Me

Funny story: Before choosing his major at UT-Austin, Tom had seen "The Empire Strikes Back" at the theater the night before. As he looked over the choices of his major for the next 4 years, he made it as far alphabetically as Aerospace Engineering, and thought, "That sounds cool!"

After graduation, finding a job, and then working in that field at the Johnson Space Center in Houston, Tom decided to "retire" and give writing a try. He paid the bills by getting a license in Massage Therapy and working for himself, and at a high-end training gym.

Twenty-five years later, with about the same amount of forethought as he gave choosing a major in college, Tom has decided to go on to a third (fourth?) profession - full stack developer!
